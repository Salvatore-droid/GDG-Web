Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join GDG Rongo University?

You can join GDG Rongo University by attending our events and meetings. We welcome all students interested in technology and development. Follow our social media channels to stay updated on our activities.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No prior experience is required! We welcome students of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced developers. Our community is about learning and growing together.

When and where are the meetings held?

We meet every Wednesday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM at the Tech Lab in Rongo University. Sometimes we have special events on weekends - check our social media for updates.

How can I become a GDG leader?

GDG leaders are selected based on their contribution to the community, technical skills, and leadership qualities. If you're interested, start by actively participating in events and projects, and express your interest to current leaders.

Ready to Join Our Community?

Become part of GDG Rongo University and connect with like-minded developers, learn new skills, and work on exciting projects.
